Exploration 1: Doctor Strange

I saw the new Marvel movie Doctor Strange (1h 55m). I was stunned by the special effects that I almost forgot it was a Marvel movie. Afterwards, I read the below review. It aligned with my thoughts about the movie entirely. I thought Benedict Cumberbatch was a great choice as Doctor Strange, and the rest of the cast fit well too. It also felt a lot like Inception (2010), with the special effects appearing to allow the characters to defy gravity and physics. As a fan of superhero movies, I was excited for Doctor Strange because it was supposed to be very different from others. I really enjoyed it and watched it more for enjoyment than for analysis.
