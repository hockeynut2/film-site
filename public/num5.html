Exploration 5: The Martian

For my 5th and final exploration, I read and then watched The Martian (2h 31m). First, I read the book, published in 2011. I found it interesting that the author, Andy Weir, originally published it one chapter at a time on his website. I enjoyed reading it so much I decided to watch the movie. It was one of my favorite sci-fi movies. It was very well done, with a main theme the stark contrast between the high-tech Hab, ships, and Earth and the wild environment of Mars that Mark Watney (Matt Damon) needed to survive. I also liked the scenes where Watney talked directly to the cameras, which made up the majority of the book.
