Exploration 4: Rogue One: A Star Wars Story

I actually watched Rogue One (2h 13m) twice in the last two weeks: once with friends during opening weekend, and again with my family last night. This may have been my favorite Star Wars movie. Yes, it was that good. The special effects were great, like the prequels, but the story was as well, like the originals. There were also great references to the original trilogy, including Darth Vader’s appearance, the Death Star, and Princess Leia. One thing I picked up was the number of establishing shots. Because Rogue One takes place on several planets (not seen in other movies) it’s very helpful to the viewers to know where the characters are going next.
